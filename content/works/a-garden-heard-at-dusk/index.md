## Program Notes

This work imagines a garden indirectly: not as a visible landscape, but as a field of partial sounds whose sources remain uncertain. Short figures pass between the instruments and return with small changes of color, pacing, or weight.

The percussionist uses a restrained collection of resonant objects. These sounds do not illustrate the natural world; they mark distances within the ensemble and make the room itself part of the music.
