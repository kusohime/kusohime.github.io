## Program Notes

*After the Mountain* is organized around the difference between approaching a form and remembering it. Dense orchestral masses appear only briefly. Most of the piece is occupied by their residue: separated pitches, softened attacks, and lines whose destination has disappeared.

The work is in one continuous movement. Its largest arrival occurs early, allowing the remaining music to consider what follows an event that cannot be reconstructed.
