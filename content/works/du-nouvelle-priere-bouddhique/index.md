## Program Notes

> buddhist furry calculating what sins to commit to get reincarnated as the animal they wish they were. like a darts player.
> — @gardenofdelete998


I composed this because I wanted to write a “<ruby>requiem<rt>安魂曲</rt></ruby>” that believes in rebirth and can be sung to non-human creatures. It is about pan-Vedic temporality, repetition and infinity (dpal be'u), immense size of numbers, manifestation, reincarnation (Wiederkunft-Samsara), contingency and eternity, “fontanelles of the new-borns” and “the ossification of Homo Duplex”, “Mount Meru in a mustard seed”, 8·10<sup>-18</sup> seconds of impermanence, and obstacle-less.

以「相信轉世，並可作用在非人類之上的安魂曲」為理念創作了這部作品。關於「<ruby>重現<rt>Wiederkunft</rt></ruby>—<ruby>輪迴<rt>Samsara</rt></ruby>」的汎吠陀時間觀、重複與無窮（盤長）、巨大數字、正念、偶然和永遠，「新生兒的囟門」和「<ruby>人<rt>Homo</rt></ruby><sub>2</sub><ruby>二<rt>Du-</rt></ruby><sub>レ</sub><ruby>重<rt>plex</rt></ruby><sub>1</sub>的骨化」、「芥子須彌」、剎那無常及無礙。
