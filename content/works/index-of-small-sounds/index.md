## Program Notes

Visitors encounter short recordings distributed across eight speakers. Some are recognizable; others have been filtered until only their rhythm or spatial character remains.

Spoken descriptions introduce sounds that are never played. The installation asks how naming, remembering, and hearing produce different kinds of evidence.
