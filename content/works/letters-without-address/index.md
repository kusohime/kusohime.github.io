## Program Notes

The text consists of salutations, interrupted statements, and closing phrases drawn from letters that were never sent. No complete narrative is given. The singer moves among several implied correspondents while the instruments preserve words and rhythms that the voice has abandoned.

Silence functions as missing text. The score asks performers to sustain the sense that a thought continues beyond what can be heard.
