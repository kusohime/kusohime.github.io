## Program Notes

The city of the title exists through documents: maps, inventories, testimonies, and songs. As the archivist attempts to reconcile them, the stage gradually fills with incompatible versions of the same streets.

Spoken and sung material overlap throughout the work. The instrumental ensemble often continues a scene after its visible action has ended, producing transitions that behave like revisions rather than changes of place.
