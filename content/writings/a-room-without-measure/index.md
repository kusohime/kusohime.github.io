The room was smaller when no one spoke. Mara wrote this in the margin of the plan, then crossed it out because the sentence could not be entered into the report.

At noon she measured the east wall again. It ended three centimeters beyond the point marked in chalk that morning. The client watched from the doorway and asked whether this was unusual.

“Only if the wall remembers,” she said.
