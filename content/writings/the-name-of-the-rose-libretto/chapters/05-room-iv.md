### <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="room-iv">Stanza IV – Venanzio &lt;Re&gt;</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Room IV - Venanzio &lt;D&gt;</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Un grande tavolo, un enorme forno, da una porta che dà sull’aia entra Salvatore.</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">A great table, an enormous oven; Salvatore enters through a door that opens onto the farmyard.</span>*

**Salvatore**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(canticchiando tra sé)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(humming to himself)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">… Femena che vendese</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">… A woman who is sold</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">como mercandia,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">as merchandise,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">no po’ nunca bon essere,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">can never be good,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">nì aver cortesia…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">nor have courtesy…</span>

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Vene.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Come.</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(Fa entrare la Ragazza del Villaggio ed esce. Dopo poco tempo entra Remigio con un involucro in mano e si avvicina alla Ragazza. Mentre comincia a spogliarla, dalle scale dello scriptorium irrompe Venanzio agonizzante chiedendo acqua)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(He lets the Village Girl enter and exits. After a short time Remigio enters with a packet in his hand and approaches the Girl. As he begins to undress her, the dying Venanzio bursts down the stairs of the scriptorium, asking for water.)</span>*

**Venanzio**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">… a … a ah …qu</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">… w … w ah …at</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">… ac … ac… qua … acqua …</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">… wa … wa… ter … water …</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(Remigio e la Ragazza scappano. Venanzio cerca di riempire d’acqua un boccale ma, facendolo cadere per terra, muore. Un’ombra, Berengario, trascina via il cadavere.)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(Remigio and the Girl flee. Venanzio tries to fill a tankard with water but, letting it fall to the ground, dies. A shadow, Berengario, drags away the corpse.)</span>*
