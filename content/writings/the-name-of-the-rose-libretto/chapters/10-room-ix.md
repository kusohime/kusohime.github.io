### <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="act-i-third-day">TERZO GIORNO</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Third Day</span>

### <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="room-ix">Stanza IX – Berengario &lt;La bemolle&gt;</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Room IX - Berengario &lt;A-flat&gt;</span>

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it"><em>Guglielmo, Adso e Severino osservano il cadavere nudo di Berengario nei balnea.</em></span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en"><em>Guglielmo, Adso, and Severino observe Berengario's naked corpse in the balnea.</em></span>

**Severino**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Il viso è gonfio, il ventre è teso.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The face is swollen, the belly is taut.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">È annegato, non vi è dubbio.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">He drowned, there is no doubt.</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Hai notato le sue dita?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Have you noticed his fingers?</span>

**Severino**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Nere.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Black.</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Come quelle di Venanzio.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Like Venanzio's.</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">E non è inchiostro.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">And it is not ink.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Aprigli la bocca,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Open his mouth,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">osservagli la lingua.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">observe his tongue.</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Maestro, è nera!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Master, it is black!</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Deve essere un veleno</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">It must be a poison</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">che, innocuo sulla pelle,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">that, harmless on the skin,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">uccide se ingerito.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">kills if ingested.</span>

**Severino**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Un momento… tempo fa</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">One moment… some time ago</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">conservavo una sostanza</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I kept a substance</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">fatta di erbe sconosciute</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">made of unknown herbs</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">che a contatto con le labbra</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">that, in contact with the lips,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">uccideva in pochi istanti.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">killed in a few instants.</span>

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Dopo un giorno di bufera</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">After a day of storm</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">che sconvolse l’ospedale</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">that convulsed the infirmary,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">non trovai più la sua ampolla</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I could no longer find its ampoule</span>

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e pensai si fosse infranta</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and I thought it had shattered,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">mescolandosi a detriti,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">mingling with debris,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">erbe e polveri disperse.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">herbs, and scattered powders.</span>

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ma adesso non escludo che qualcuno</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">But now I cannot exclude that someone</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">possa avere approfittato del momento</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">may have taken advantage of the moment</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">per nascondere il suo furto.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">to conceal his theft.</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Chi sapeva dell’ampolla?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Who knew about the ampoule?</span>

**Severino**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Di sicuro Abbone,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Certainly Abbone,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ma anche Malachia</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">but also Malachia</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e chissà quanti altri.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and who knows how many others.</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Maestro… la terza tromba!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Master… the third trumpet!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">La morte vien per acqua!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Death comes by water!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">La quarta vien dal cielo?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The fourth comes from the sky?</span>

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it"><em>(trovando nel saio di Berengario gli occhiali di Guglielmo)</em></span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en"><em>(Finding Guglielmo's spectacles in Berengario's habit.)</em></span>

**Severino**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Sono tuoi, vero?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">These are yours, are they not?</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ecco chi è stato!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">So it was he!</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ma prima di annegare nella vasca</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">But before drowning in the tub</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">è stato altrove</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">he was somewhere else</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e ha nascosto il nostro libro.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and hid our book.</span>

**Severino**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Quale libro?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">What book?</span>

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it"><em>(inforca gli occhiali, prende la pergamena di Venanzio e inizia a leggere:)</em></span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en"><em>(He puts on the spectacles, takes Venanzio's parchment, and begins to read:)</em></span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">“La pietra svergognata rotola per la pianura…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">“The shameless stone rolls across the plain…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">a essi le cicale canteranno da terra…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">to them the cicadas shall sing from the earth…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Il veleno tremendo che dà la purificazione…”</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The terrible poison that gives purification…”</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">E cosa vuol dire?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">And what does it mean?</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Qualcosa che conosco ma</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Something I know but</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">non riesco a ricordare.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">cannot manage to remember.</span>

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it"><em>(Entra Abbone, scuro in volto)</em></span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en"><em>(Abbone enters, dark of countenance.)</em></span>

**Abbone**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Sono arrivati i legati papali</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The papal legates have arrived</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e l’autore dei delitti</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and the author of the crimes</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">è ancora ignoto.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">is still unknown.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Non posso rischiare</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I cannot risk</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">che pensino a un complotto.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">their thinking it a conspiracy.</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Capisco.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I understand.</span>

**Abbone**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">E non è tutto,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">And that is not all,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">li scorta una compagnia di arcieri</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">a company of archers escorts them</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">agli ordini di un inquisitore:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">under the orders of an inquisitor:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Bernardo Gui.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Bernardo Gui.</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="en">Zounds odsbodikins!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Good heavens!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Quel martello degli eretici!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">That hammer of heretics!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">La cosa non mi piace.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I do not like this.</span>
