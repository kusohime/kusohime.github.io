## <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="act-ii">Atto II</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Act II</span>

### <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="act-ii-fourth-day">QUARTO GIORNO</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Fourth Day</span>

### <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="room-xiii">Stanza XIII – Il cuore &lt;Fa diesis&gt;</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Room XIII – The Heart &lt;F-sharp&gt;</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Di notte, nelle cucine. Adso giace a terra svenuto accanto all’involto.</span>*<br />
*<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">At night, in the kitchens. Adso lies unconscious on the ground beside the bundle.</span>*

**Guglielmo**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(bagnandogli il volto)</span>*<br />
*<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(wetting his face)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Su su ragazzo.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Come, come, boy.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Cos’è successo?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">What happened?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Giri di notte?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Roaming at night?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Rubi frattaglie?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Stealing offal?</span>

**Adso**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(riprendendosi e indicando il cuore nell’involto)</span>*<br />
*<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(coming to and pointing to the heart in the bundle)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">… un nuovo delitto!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">… a new crime!</span>

**Guglielmo**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(sorridendo)</span>*<br />
*<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(smiling)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ti sembra un cuore d’uomo?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Does it look like a human heart to you?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Non vedi che è di bue?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Don’t you see that it is an ox’s?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Perché è tra le tue mani?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Why is it in your hands?</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Non capisco.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I don’t understand.</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Piuttosto dimmi…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Rather, tell me…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Mentre ti cercavo</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">While I was looking for you</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ho visto un’ombra</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I saw a shadow</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">che fuggiva…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">that was fleeing…</span>

**Adso**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(turbato)</span>*<br />
*<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(perturbed)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">… è fuggita?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">… she fled?</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ma t’ha lasciato il cuore!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">But she left you the heart!</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">… come l’aurora,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">… like the dawn,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">… come la luna,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">… like the moon,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">bella e terribile…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">beautiful and terrible…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Padre, ho peccato!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Father, I have sinned!</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Si dice che la donna</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">It is said that woman</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">sia più amara della morte,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">is more bitter than death,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">che il suo cuore sia una rete,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">that her heart is a net,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">le sue mani siano funi:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">her hands are ropes:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ma la donna non può essere</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">But woman cannot be</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">vascello del demonio</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">a vessel of the Devil</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">se il Signore ha scelto</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">if the Lord chose</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">di incarnarsi nel suo ventre.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">to incarnate in her womb.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Adso, tu hai peccato</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Adso, you have sinned</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">contro i tuoi doveri,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">against your duties,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ma l’averlo fatto</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">but having done it</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">non è così mostruoso.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">is not so monstrous.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">E quindi vai con Dio,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">And so go with God,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">non se ne parli più.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">let it be spoken of no more.</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(guardando l’involto)</span>*<br />
*<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(looking at the bundle)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Se si concedeva</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">If she gave herself</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">per un pacco di rognoni</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">for a packet of kidneys,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">la ragazza si incontrava</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">the girl was meeting</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">con un uomo brutto e vecchio.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">with an ugly old man.</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Una meretrice!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">A harlot!</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Una ragazza povera;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">A poor girl;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">se potesse, si darebbe</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">if she could, she would give herself</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">solo per amore.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">only for love.</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Chi è quest’uomo brutto e vecchio?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Who is this ugly old man?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Salvatore?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Salvatore?</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">O il Cellario.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Or the Cellarer.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Li ho visti confabulare.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I saw them conferring.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Dobbiamo interrogarli.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">We must interrogate them.</span>
