### <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="act-ii-sixth-day">SESTO GIORNO</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Sixth Day</span>


### <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="room-xx">Stanza XX – Sederunt principes &lt;Re&gt;</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Room XX – <em>Sederunt principes</em> &lt;D&gt;</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">In chiesa.</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">In the church.</span>*

**Voce di Adso vecchio**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Dopo un sonno agitato,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">After a troubled sleep,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">quell’ultima parte della notte</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">during that last part of the night</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">scendemmo in chiesa.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">we went down to the church.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Finito il Mattutino,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">When Matins was over,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">per prepararsi alla messa natalizia</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">to prepare for the Christmas Mass</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">l’abate invitò a intonare il Sederunt.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">the Abbot invited us to intone the Sederunt.</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(I monaci intonano il Sederunt principes. Guglielmo indica il posto vuoto accanto a Jorge)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(The monks intone the Sederunt principes. Guglielmo indicates the empty place beside Jorge.)</span>*

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Dove sarà il bibliotecario?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Where can the librarian be?</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Malachia… Di sicuro ha il libro.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Malachia… He certainly has the book.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Se l’autore dei delitti non è lui,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">If he is not the author of the crimes,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">potrebbe non conoscerne i pericoli…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">he might not know its dangers…</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(Durante il canto, rientra Malachia e si siede al suo posto accanto a Jorge. Monaci veglianti verificano che nessuno dorma: scosso da uno di questi, Malachia cade al suolo)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(During the chant, Malachia returns and sits in his place beside Jorge. Vigilant monks verify that no one is sleeping: shaken by one of them, Malachia falls to the floor.)</span>*

**Monaci**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">…MALACHIA! MALACHIA!!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">…MALACHIA! MALACHIA!!</span>

**Malachia**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(soffocando, con voce spezzata)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(choking, in a broken voice)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Me lo aveva detto… Davvero…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">He had told me… Truly…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Aveva il potere di mille scorpioni…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">It had the power of a thousand scorpions…</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Chi te l’aveva detto? Chi?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Who told you? Who?</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(Malachia tenta di rispondere ma, sconvolto da un gran tremito, muore)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(Malachia tries to reply but, convulsed by a great tremor, dies.)</span>*

**Guglielmo**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(vedendo Gui dietro l’Abate)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(seeing Gui behind the Abbot)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Signor Bernardo,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Lord Bernardo,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">chi ha ucciso costui</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">who killed this man</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">se avete preso gli assassini?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">if you have caught the murderers?</span>

**Gui**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Non ho mai detto</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I never said</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">di averli presi tutti.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I had caught them all.</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(Si sente singhiozzare: è Jorge)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(Sobbing is heard: it is Jorge.)</span>*

**Jorge**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Non finirà mai…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">It will never end…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">È scritto nell’Apocalisse.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">It is written in the Apocalypse.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">L’Anticristo sta arrivando,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The Antichrist is coming,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">la sua testa fuoco ardente,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">his head is burning fire,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">un occhio rosso sangue,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">one eye blood-red,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">un altro verde bile,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">the other bile-green,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">le sue pupille bianche,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">his pupils white,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">il suo labbro avvelenato…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">his lip poisoned…</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Pare il suo ritratto.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">It resembles his portrait.</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(Adso trattiene una risata)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(Adso suppresses a laugh.)</span>*

**Jorge**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">E vi dico:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">And I tell you:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">chi rompe i sigilli</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">whoever breaks the seals</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">dei libri proibiti</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">of forbidden books</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">conoscerà la collera</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">will know the wrath</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">di Dio e la sua vendetta.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">of God and his vengeance.</span>

**Guglielmo**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(chinandosi sul cadavere)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(bending over the corpse)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">I polpastrelli della destra sono scuri.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The fingertips of the right hand are dark.</span>

**Abbone**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(vedendo Guglielmo chino su Malachia)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(seeing Guglielmo bent over Malachia)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">State ancora inquisendo?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Are you still investigating?</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Non più. Devo parlarvi.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">No longer. I must speak with you.</span>
