### <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="room-xxi">Stanza XXI – Un libro &lt;Si bemolle&gt;</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Room XXI – A Book &lt;B-flat&gt;</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">In una cella di monastero.</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">In a monastic cell.</span>*

**Abbone**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Sei giorni sono passati,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Six days have passed,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">cinque monaci sono morti</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">five monks have died</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e due sono agli arresti.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and two are under arrest.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">L’abbazia è in fermento,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The Abbey is in ferment,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ed è anche colpa vostra.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and it is also your fault.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Cosa mai avete scoperto?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">What have you discovered?</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">È vero, vi ho deluso.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">It is true, I have disappointed you.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ma una cosa l’ho capita:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">But one thing I have understood:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">tutti sanno degli illeciti rapporti</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">everyone knows of the illicit relations</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">tra Adelmo, Berengario e Malachia…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">among Adelmo, Berengario, and Malachia…</span>

**Abbone**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(con stupore)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(with astonishment)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Cosa dite?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">What are you saying?</span>

**Guglielmo**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(continuando)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(continuing)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">… ma la chiave delle morti era un’altra:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">… but the key to the deaths was something else:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">il furto di un libro nascosto in finis Africae.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">the theft of a book hidden in *finis Africae*.</span>

**Abbone**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Come sapete del finis Africae?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">How do you know of the *finis Africae*?</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Come sapevo del vostro cavallo?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">As I knew of your horse.</span>

**Abbone**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Continuate…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Continue…</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">I delitti servivano</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The crimes served</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">a proteggere quel libro.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">to protect that book.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Chi sapeva dei segreti</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Whoever knew the secrets</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">della biblioteca è morto.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">of the library is dead.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Restate solo voi.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Only you remain.</span>

**Abbone**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Volete insinuare…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">You mean to insinuate…</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Che siete l’ultimo a sapere</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">That you are the last to know</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e forse il prossimo a morire,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and perhaps the next to die,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">a meno che non mi diciate</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">unless you tell me</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">chi si cela dietro al libro.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">who conceals himself behind the book.</span>

**Abbone**  

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(ad Adso)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(to Adso)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Fa freddo qui.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">It is cold here.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ragazzo,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Boy,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">quante cose inquietanti</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">how many disquieting things</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">devi aver sentito…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">you must have heard…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Non ti preoccupare, guarda:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Do not worry, look:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">riconosci questo anello?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">do you recognize this ring?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ecco l’ametista, specchio di umiltà;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Here is the amethyst, mirror of humility;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ecco il calcedonio, segno di carità;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">here is the chalcedony, sign of charity;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ecco il diaspro, simbolo di fede;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">here is the jasper, symbol of faith;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ecco la sardonica, insegna di martirio;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">here is the sardonyx, emblem of martyrdom;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ecco lo zaffiro, figura di speranza;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">here is the sapphire, figure of hope;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ecco il berillo, luce di dottrina:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">here is the beryl, light of doctrine:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">giura su queste pietre,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">swear on these stones,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">giura su questo anello,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">swear on this ring,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">sigilla le tue labbra</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">seal your lips</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e dimentica.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and forget.</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(Adso fa per inchinarsi, ma viene bloccato da Guglielmo)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(Adso begins to bow, but Guglielmo stops him.)</span>*

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Cosa c’entra il ragazzo?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">What has the boy to do with this?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Volete forse che baci anch’io l’anello?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Do you perhaps want me too to kiss the ring?</span>

**Abbone**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Oh voi…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Oh, you…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">non mi aspetto che un frate mendicante</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I do not expect a mendicant friar</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">comprenda.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">to comprehend.</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Vi ho posto una domanda,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I asked you a question,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">vi ho avvertito di un pericolo,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I warned you of a danger,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">vi ho chiesto: fate un nome!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I asked you: give me a name!</span>

**Abbone**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Quel che dite sono fole,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The things you say are idle tales,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">illazioni senza senso.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">senseless conjectures.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">La vostra missione è terminata,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Your mission is terminated,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">le legazioni hanno lasciato l’abbazia,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">the legations have left the Abbey;</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">vi do licenza di partire domattina.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I give you leave to depart tomorrow morning.</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(Abbone si allontana)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(Abbone leaves.)</span>*

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Bastardo di un feudatario,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Bastard of a feudal lord,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">pavone, otre gonfiato.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">peacock, inflated wineskin.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Gli ammazzano i monaci</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">They murder his monks</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e lui pensa all’abbazia!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and he thinks of the Abbey!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Solo adesso ha capito</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Only now has he understood,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e sarà il prossimo a morire.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and he will be the next to die.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ma ormai la sfida è mia:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">But by now the challenge is mine:</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">entro domattina</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">before tomorrow morning</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">devo sapere.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I must know.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Devo.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">I must.</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Le legazioni hanno lasciato l’abbazia</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The legations have left the Abbey,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">trascinandoti lontano e per sempre.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">dragging you far away and forever.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Povera ragazza, ti portano al rogo,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Poor girl, they are taking you to the stake,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">e non ho potuto e non posso fare niente.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">and I could do nothing and can do nothing.</span>
