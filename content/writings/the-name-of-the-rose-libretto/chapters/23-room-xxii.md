### <span class="libretto-source" lang="it" id="room-xxii">Stanza XXII – L’enigma svelato &lt;Do diesis&gt;</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Room XXII – The Enigma Unveiled &lt;C-sharp&gt;</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Di fronte allo specchio della biblioteca Adso e Guglielmo si interrogano sul modo in cui entrare nel finis Africae. Dalle feritoie si sente rumore di vento, da dietro a un muro dei colpi.</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">In front of the library mirror, Adso and Guglielmo question how to enter the finis Africae. The sound of wind is heard through the arrow slits, and blows from behind a wall.</span>*

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Cos’è stato?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">What was that?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Maestro, sentite?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Master, do you hear?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Qualcuno batte…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Someone is knocking…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ancora…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Again…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">È chiuso qui dentro…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">He is shut in here…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Come è finito dietro al muro?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">How did he end up behind the wall?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Chi sarà?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Who can it be?</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">La seconda persona</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The second person</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">che la prima ha intrappolato.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">whom the first has entrapped.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Chi sia lo scopriremo</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Who he is, we shall discover</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">aprendo questa porta.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">by opening this door.</span>

*<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">(rileggendo il messaggio di Venanzio)</span><br /><span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">(rereading Venanzio's message)</span>*

<span class="libretto-source" lang="la">“Primum et septimum de quatuor”.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">“The first and the seventh of the four.”</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">La mano sopra l’idolo</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The hand above the idol</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">opera sul primo e il settimo dei quattro.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">operates upon the first and the seventh of the four.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Cosa intendeva Venanzio?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">What did Venanzio mean?</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Ci vorrebbe un incantesimo</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">It would take an incantation</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">come quelli di Salvatore!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">like Salvatore's!</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Logica, Adso, numeri.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Logic, Adso, numbers.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Uno… sette… quattro…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">One… seven… four…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Siamo alla sesta tromba…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">We are at the sixth trumpet…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Un terzo delle acque…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">A third of the waters…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">un terzo del sangue,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">a third of the blood,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">un terzo del cielo…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">a third of the sky…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">un ter…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">a thir…</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Un terzo del cavallo…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">A third of the horse…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="la">Tertius equi!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Third horse!</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Cosa?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">What?</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Niente,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Nothing;</span><br />

<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">the other day Salvatore,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">indicandomi un cavallo,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">indicating a horse to me,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">il terzo della fila,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">the third in the row,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ha detto tertius equi.</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">said *tertius equi*.</span>

**Guglielmo**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="la">Equi?</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Horse?</span>

**Adso**  
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Sì, ma in buon latino</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Yes, but in good Latin</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="la">Tertius equi</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">Third horse</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">non significa</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">does not signify</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">il terzo cavallo</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">the third horse</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ma un terzo del cavallo,</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">but a third of the horse,</span><br />

<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">E-Qu-U…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">ma è solo una sciocchezza!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">but it is only nonsense!</span>

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">La scritta… il versetto…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">The inscription… the verse…</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Dio ti benedica!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-translation" lang="en">God bless you!</span><br />
<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">Adso, basta premere</span><br />

<span class="libretto-source" lang="it">E non temere, Dio è con noi.</span><br />
